Sponsor TEDxSanLuisObispo.

We are currently seeking both in-kind and financial contributions from organizations and departments who share our vision of bringing out San Luis Obispo’s best and brightest for the benefit of the community. We intend to bring TEDxSanLuisObispo to the city year after year, and will be putting all excess donations and profits towards a fund for the next year’s TEDx. A partnership with TEDx and our organizing team is a gesture in support of collaboration, learning, and positive growth among the community.
